Simple checklist for responsible use of social media

Do’s
* Think before posting – make sure your content is appropriate and respectful 
* Use strong passwords and enable two-factor authentication 
* Verify information before sharing to avoid spreading misinformation 
* Adjust privacy settings to control who can see your content 
* Maintain a positive digital footprint that reflects your personality and goals 
Don’ts
* Do not share personal and sensitive information like OTP, passwords and address.
* Don’t post in anger or frustration, it can harm your reputation 
* Don’t engage in cyberbullying or trolling 
* Don’t accept requests from unknown people blindly 
* Don’t overshare your daily life or location publicly
