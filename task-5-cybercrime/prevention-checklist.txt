Stay Safe Online

* Use strong, unique passwords for each account 
*  Turn on two-factor authentication (2FA) for email, social media, and banking apps 
*  Think before clicking links in emails, WhatsApp, or SMS — especially unknown ones 
*  Keep your apps and phone updated to avoid security vulnerabilities 
*  Limit what you share online (college ID, address, phone number, location, etc.) 
*  Only download apps from trusted sources like the Google Play Store or App Store 
* Log out from shared and public computers like in libraries and labs
*  Never share your UPI PIN or OTP with anyone. No bank or service will ever ask for it 
* Don’t approve unknown payment requests, receiving money never requires entering your PIN 
*  Double-check UPI IDs and names before sending money 
* Avoid scanning random QR codes sent by unknown people
   

If Something Goes Wrong
* Immediately report the issue on National Cyber Crime Portal (cybercrime.gov.in) 
* Call Helpline: 1930 as soon as possible to report financial fraud
